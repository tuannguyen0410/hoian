INTINERARY

Saigon is claimed to be the city that never stop eating. Saigonese usually find themselves enjoy eating even at midnight. So, what would be a better idea than exploring this city via a fun motorbike ride and visiting best local eateries? That’s why we’d love to invite you to come with us on the “SAIGON STREET FOOD TOUR”.

We try to serve you street food dishes that you have never had a chance to taste if you are not Vietnamese local people. Moreover, different from most other food tours, we also will not order any weird cuisines such as: insects, bugs, raw foods, jungle animal foods or pet foods and Pho (Rice Noodle soup with beef from the North Vietnam), Broken Rice, Bun Bo (Beef Rice Noodles from Middle Vietnam) or even Vietnamese Banh Mi (Bread) is not stand in our menu because you can find them easily on every Saigon street

INCLUSION

• A fun Motorbike ride during the tour
• 6 + delicious food & drink tastings as mentioned in the program (enough for big late dinner)
• Experienced and friendly local tour guide
• A chilled Saigon beer and a non-alcoholic drink (sugarcane juice) at the local place
• Pick up and drop off service in the tour ( only hotels in Saigon city center – District 1,3). Extra from 3 – 5$ for outside area
• Complimentary basic medical insurance
